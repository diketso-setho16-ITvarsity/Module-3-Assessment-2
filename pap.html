Pap 'n Vleis

Cost: R65.00

Est Min: 20 Mins

About

Traditional Pap & Vleis is a combination of Mzantsi's Boerewors and Mealie meal. This is the perfect diversity meal as the ingredients used in this dish represent all the South African cultures. Mealie Meal also well known as “Pap” in many parts of Mzantsi and is a traditional porridge made from ground maize.

Ingredients

  • Maize Pap
  • Gravy
  • Beef
  • Wors
